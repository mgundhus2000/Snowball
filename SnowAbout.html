About Us

Norwegian old field organization

The Norwegian old field association was established in 1990 and is Norway’s youngest nature and environmental protection organization mainly aimed on the restoration of forgotten fields. We have many local teams that include several municipalities. These local teams work with nature especially in consideration of restoring fields and other related issues in their areas.
The Norwegian old fields organization works actively and effectively in cooperation with the government to secure and restore old and no longer usable fields. This is because by restoring and maintaining abandoned fileds back into a usable state we can reduce the amount of forested area that we cut down or burn to make new fields.

International commitment

Norway’s old field conservation is involved in several international networks. They represent Norway in the world particularly in restoring fields. They also collaborate international with related issues.
Norway’s old field collaborates with many European countries who have interest restoring old field to cereal fruitful. Their philosophy is that the best way to develop solutions is to collaborate with experienced actors who can implement these solutions. its our result that giving us access to international partnership.
